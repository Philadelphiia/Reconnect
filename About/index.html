ABOUT US

  • Idemudia Philip
  • 23/05/2022 3:43pm

ReconnectNaija came into the internet birthed from the idea of Idemudia Philip, a Law student of Ambrose Alli University during the period of one of the notorious strike action of the Academic Staff Union of Universities (ASUU) that occured sometime in 2022, with the aim to further actualize his idea, he wireframed, designed and wrote the codes that birthed ReconnectNaija with the intent to use it as a personal blog to share his knowledge of the Law and all other topics and subject matters he has vast knowledge of and knows will be helpful to his users/visitors.

ReconnectNaija started with a total of 5 categories namely Law, News, Government, Article and Education.

ReconnectNaija was launched in June 2022 under the domain name ReconnectNaija.com and it's operation started and since then the blog has been actively posting contents, articles, news and updating it's visitors on latest news, captivating as well as intuitive articles.

Since it's launch, ReconnectNaija has been having a large number of unique visitors/users that frequently visit our site and also subscribe to get our monthly Newsletter and updates.
