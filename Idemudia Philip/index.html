ABOUT IDEMUDIA PHILIP

  • Idemudia Philip
  • 23/05/2022 3:43pm

Idemudia Philip is the founder of and publisher for ReconnectNaija.com, he is currently a Law student of Ambrose Alli University Ekpoma, Edo State, he also happens to be the Class Governor of his class of over 300 Phenomenal Law students.

Philip learnt and took Web Development courses during the notorious ASUU strike of 2022 that lasted for over 3 months, and further used his newly acquired web development/design skills to create ReconnectNaija.com, a personal blog where he post articles and contents on Law, News, Education and the Government as well as other subject matters his visitors will be interested in and can learn from.

Philip writes his articles with the skills he acquired being a legal writer, he is notorious for explaining concepts in his writing to the barest minimum there explaining a very difficult concept to be easily understood by his readers/audience.

Idemudia Philip is somewhat of a time manager as he somehow finds time to juggle between his personal life, academics, his duties as a Class Governor and also the cumbersome job of being a Blogger/Publisher/Content creator/Writer not forgetting being a meme supplier to his Whatsapp audience.
